# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Movie enthusiasts looking for a premium, fast, and cinematic desktop library to organize their local files, explore catalog content, and manage watchlists from their desktop in a relaxed, dimmed ambient environment.

## Product Purpose

To serve as a high-performance, immersive, and visually stunning movie and TV show catalog workspace that works locally and feels premium.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, Minimalist, Premium. Warm, dark-dimmed cinematic vibes.

## Anti-references

- SaaS cliché dashboards with heavy borders.
- Flat, boring tables.
- Overly bright, neon colors.
- Cluttered grid layouts.

## Design Principles

- **Cinematic Focus**: Draw all attention to rich media posters and metadata, letting the content be the main hero.
- **Deep Minimalist Harmony**: Use tinted dark neutrals and elegant, borderless (or extremely subtle) structures rather than heavy borders or grid lines.
- **Performance First**: Zero unnecessary layout shift or visual clutter; direct, responsive rendering.
- **Respect Motion Preferences**: Zero gratuitous motion or animations to maintain a quiet, fast, and accessible user experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Respect reduced motion settings (0 animations).
- High contrast readable text over dark backgrounds.
- Clear typographic hierarchy for relaxed night viewing.
